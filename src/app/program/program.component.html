Liberal Arts Programs

MIT's Liberal Arts Program today is running one Bachelor degree program, one post-graduate diploma, and four Master degree programs, namely Bachelor of Arts/Science in major fields of studies; Post-graduate Diploma in Community Development; Master of Arts in Community Development (MACD), Master of Arts in Peace Studies (MAPS), Master of Arts in Interfaith Dialogue (MAID) and Master of Arts in Gender Studies (MAGS).
