Academic Department

In Myanmar Institute of Theology, we have several departments for each academic program: Theology Program and Liberal Art Program. Each departments in its respective academic program composes with one another to form academic subjects for students who are learning in either program. By previewing the departments and subjects, students can be more incisive and clear to make a final decision on which type of program they want to take part and its respective subjects that would suit the students' expectation.

MIT's Expected Outcomes

1. To become a leading and premier Christian University in Myanmar and in Asia
2. To produce good national leaders and distinct academicians in various fields
3. To become an international-level Christian University to admit international students
4. To provide a broad-based and all round liberal arts education that is based on the Christian principles and contextual needs.
5. To become a government recognized University
6. To network with other Universities in Myanmar and abroad
