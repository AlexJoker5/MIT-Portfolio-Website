Public Events and Campaign of MIT

Myanmar Institute of Theology has several events for students as well as many public campaigns. These events and campaigns help the students grow their ethical behavior, moral practice as well as the ability of liberal thinking and communication with one another to produce a greater and better community and their respective society.

Fun - Fete

In December, MIT holds its Annual Fun - Fete. This is partly to raise funds for the schools but the principle aim is to develop the leadership and organizational qualities of its students as well as to instill in them the sense of sharing responsibilities, working in unity and having fellowship and understanding towards one another.

Other Fellowships

Many ethnic and religious groups represented at MIT provide diversity and color to student life. However, any social group / club / fellowship are to be formed only with the permission of the school. Existing groups are required to register with the school.
