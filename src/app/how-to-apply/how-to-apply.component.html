How To Apply

Apply to Myanmar Institute of Theology

You can apply to Myanmar Institute of Theology using our online form or through a local agent. You can start filling in the form today and save it for later if you don't have all the information available right now. You can also submit your supporting documents with your form. If you are applying through an agent, please contact them for an application form.
When you apply with us directly our team of dedicated Student Enrolment Advisors will contact you once your application has been received and reviewed and they will guide you every step of the way until you arrive to study with us.

Why You Should Apply to MIT

The objectives of Myanmar Institute of Theology include producing competent and faithful young leaders and scholars at national and international levels. Moreover, it has so much networking with universities, colleges and other academic institutions within and outside Myanmar. Those who love adventures, challenges and so on, will easily be fit into the atmosphere of the School.
