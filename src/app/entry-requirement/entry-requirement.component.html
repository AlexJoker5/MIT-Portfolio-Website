Entry Requirements for Liberal Arts Program

Anyone regardless of religion, faith, age, race and gender who has the following criteria is eligible to apply for Bachelor Degree Program.

  1. Matriculation passed (A List) or an equivalent qualification (Agricultural/Technical High School passed); GCE 'O' level passed.
  2. Three-hour English test (Entrance examination passed)
  3. be of good health (An up-to-date Medical Report)

Application Form Information for Liberal Arts Program


  1. Application forms are available at LAP Office starting from May.
  2. Deadline for application is the last Friday of August, 5:00 PM every year.
  3. Entrance exam will be held on Saturday, the second week of September.
  4. One recommendation from teacher/principal of the community or advisor. The person recommending must not be from one's family. (Form attached; Official seal required)
  5. One recommendation from pastor/elder of the community or advisor. The person recommending must not be from one's family. (Form attached; Official seal required)
  6. To submit Photo-copy of Matriculation Certificate and to produce the Original Certificate at the Office for Entrance Examination.
  7. To submit Photo-copy of Official Matriculation Transcript (Marks) and to produce the Original for Entrance Examination.
  8. One page essay on “My Life” which should include information about your family, home, school, hobbies and activities.
  9. Two recent passport size photos (taken within six months)
